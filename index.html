Why You Should Never Order 12 Chicken Nuggets

Aug 1, 2021 · 7 min read

Ordering 12 chicken nuggets is rarely a smart choice because it's deliberately designed as a psychological "middle trap": 6 feels too small, 20 looks like a much better deal for just a little more money, and 12 sits in between with the worst value per nugget, neither truly filling nor economical, creating the illusion of moderation while quietly nudging you into overpaying—this isn't a food decision, it's pricing psychology at work.
